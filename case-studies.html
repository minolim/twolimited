Data Unification Taxonomy Ad Ops Reporting

The Challenge

A fragmented data landscape across dozens of platforms and markets.

Dentsu managed Microsoft's media planning, buying, and reporting across multiple countries and dozens of digital platforms, search, social, programmatic, native, video. Each platform had its own naming conventions, its own dashboards, and its own way of reporting performance. Campaign data lived in siloed spreadsheets, PowerPoint decks, and platform-native UIs. There was no single view of truth, and generating a cross-platform performance report required days of manual extraction and reconciliation.

Account teams spent enormous time on brief processing, purchase order creation, and trafficking, work that was repetitive, error-prone, and took senior strategists away from actual strategy.

What We Did

Built an exclusive data model that unified every source into one truth.

Two designed and deployed a proprietary data model that connected campaign data from every single platform, Google Ads, Meta, DV360, LinkedIn, Twitter, programmatic DSPs, and more, into a unified, normalised data architecture. Each campaign, line item, and creative was tagged with a standardised taxonomy and a unique identifier, making cross-platform comparison instant and accurate.

We built automated extraction pipelines that pulled data from dirty, inconsistent sources, including PowerPoint media plans and Excel billing sheets, and cleaned, mapped, and loaded them into a structured database. Ad ops teams received automated trafficking sheets with pre-populated taxonomy. Account teams submitted briefs through a structured workflow that auto-generated purchase orders and routed them for approval. Finance reconciliation became automatic.

Unified Data Model

Exclusive schema connecting all platforms with unique IDs and standardised taxonomy.

Dirty Source Extraction

Automated parsing of PPTX media plans and Excel files into clean, database-ready structures.

Trafficking & PO Automation

Briefs auto-generated trafficking sheets for ad ops and routed purchase orders for approval.

Performance Marketing A/B Optimisation Real-time Dashboards

The Challenge

High-tempo performance campaigns with zero room for error.

Unibet's performance marketing engine runs at extraordinary speed, hundreds of campaigns live at any time across paid search, social, and programmatic, spanning multiple regulated markets. The team needed to move fast, but campaign setup was bottlenecked by manual trafficking, inconsistent naming conventions, and fragmented reporting across platforms. Understanding true cost per acquisition, lifetime value, and ROAS by channel was taking days, not minutes.

A/B testing was ad hoc, with no systematic way to track variants, allocate spend to winners, or learn from historical performance. Creative and media insights lived in separate silos, making optimisation reactive rather than proactive.

What We Did

Built a performance automation layer from spend to optimisation.

Two built an end-to-end performance automation system for Unibet. At the foundation, we deployed our data extraction layer, pulling real-time spend, conversion, and revenue data from every active platform and normalising it into a unified performance model with granular taxonomy down to the creative variant level.

On top of that, we built automated A/B optimisation workflows: the system tracked performance of creative and audience variants in real time, flagged underperformers, and surfaced recommendations for budget reallocation. Campaign setup was automated with standardised taxonomy and pre-built trafficking templates, cutting launch time dramatically. Account teams could submit briefs through a structured intake flow that auto-generated media plans, trafficking sheets, and purchase orders.

Real-time dashboards gave the performance team instant visibility into CPA, ROAS, and LTV by channel, market, and campaign, updated hourly, not weekly.

Key Capabilities Delivered

A/B Optimisation Engine

Real-time variant tracking with automated spend reallocation to top performers.

Real-time Performance

Hourly-updated dashboards with CPA, ROAS, and LTV by channel, market, and campaign.

Speed to Market

Automated campaign setup, taxonomy, and trafficking cut launch time by over 60%.
